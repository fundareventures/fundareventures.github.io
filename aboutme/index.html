Meet Janice "Coach J" Chen

I'm passionate about helping people move from financial stress to financial confidence.

Through personalized one-on-one coaching, I support individuals looking to get organized, pay off debt, save purposefully, and invest in their futures.

I also partner with employers who want to offer meaningful financial wellness programs to their teams — improving retention, morale, and focus by reducing financial stress at the root.

My coaching philosophy is simple: true financial change comes from understanding both the technical skills and the mindset behind money decisions. I believe in meeting people where they are — offering collaborative, judgment-free support with practical, actionable steps that fit their real lives.
