Meet Janice "Coach J" Chen

I founded Coach J Financial and Fundare Ventures with a clear mission: to help individuals and businesses achieve financial clarity, confidence, and long-term success through education, actionable strategies, and personal support.

Whether you’re navigating personal budgeting challenges or looking to boost employee wellness through workplace programs, I’m here to make financial literacy approachable — and achievable.

My Coaching Philosophy

I believe that true financial change comes from understanding both the technical skills and the mindset behind money decisions.

My coaching style is collaborative, supportive, and focused on practical steps — not judgment. Together, we’ll build a plan that fits your life and empowers you for the future.
