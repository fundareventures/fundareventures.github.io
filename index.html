Working with small business owners

Coaching for founders, freelancers, and solo owners

Small business ownership blurs the line between “work” and “life” money. Coaching creates a space to organize the moving pieces, reduce stress, and make decisions that support both your business and your personal priorities.

Together, we look at how cash flows in and out of your business, how you pay yourself, and what needs to be set aside for taxes, emergencies, and growth. The goal is to give you clarity and a repeatable process—not a complicated spreadsheet you never open again.

  • Foundations and structure. Clarify how money moves through your business, set up simple systems, and separate business and personal finances in a way that feels manageable.
  • Paying yourself with intention. Create a plan for consistent owner pay that accounts for seasonality, taxes, and your household needs.
  • Planning for the future. Connect today’s decisions—pricing, hiring, benefits, and savings—to the long-term life you’re building for yourself and the people you care about.
