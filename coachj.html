For Small Business Owners

Your business is a huge part of your life—and your money. This coaching track is designed for founders, freelancers, and solo owners who want their business finances and personal finances to work together instead of against each other.

What we can work on

  • Cash flow and uneven income.  Build a plan for seasonality so you can pay yourself consistently.
  • Separating business and personal money.  Create simple systems for accounts, transfers, and tracking that you will actually use.
  • Taxes, savings, and reinvestment.  Decide what to set aside for taxes, emergencies, and growth so you are not guessing.
  • Using your business to support your life.  Connect your pricing, workload, and benefits to the life you want for yourself and your family.

How coaching works for owners

We start with a clear picture of your current numbers—what is coming in, what is going out, and what feels stressful. From there, we build a simple playbook for how money moves through your business and into your household.

You will leave each session with 1–3 concrete action steps and an understanding of why those steps matter, so you can keep moving forward even when things get busy.
